## üî• Key Principles Applied

1. **Specificity Over Generality**
   - Not "fast response" ‚Üí "3am lead gets response in 60 seconds"
   - Not "more revenue" ‚Üí "20% increase in Week 1, that's $12K/month"

2. **Numbers Tell Stories**
   - Every claim backed by a specific metric
   - Timelines included (Week 1, 26 days, 5 years)

3. **Before/After Contrast**
   - Show the pain they're living right now
   - Paint the relief of the solution

4. **Tangible Scenarios**
   - "3am lead" not "night-time inquiries"
   - "7 emails back and forth" not "scheduling friction"

5. **Outcomes Over Features**
   - Not what you build ‚Üí what changes for them
   - Not your process ‚Üí their results
